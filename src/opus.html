Opus values

Ingrediets quality

To prepare a really tasty dessert, you just need the best and natural products! You will never find butter substitutes, chemical preservatives or flavor enhancers in our desserts.

Craft production

Confectionery Opus is a full-cycle production. Selection and purchase of raw materials, search and daily improvement of recipes, attention to detail and daily control of every process in the kitchen.

Service

Every customer is important - for the Opus team, this is not a word, but a daily choice! We respect and love our customers and see that it is reciprocated, as most become regulars.
